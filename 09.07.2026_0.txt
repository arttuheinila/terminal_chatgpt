2. **Deadlifts**  
   - **Key Benefits**: Builds posterior chain strength (back, glutes, hamstrings) and enhances grip strength.
   - **Execution**: Stand with feet hip-width apart, barbell over mid-foot. Bend at hips and knees to grip the bar, keep back straight, lift by extending hips and knees simultaneously.
   - **Variations**: Conventional, sumo, Romanian.

3. **Push-Ups**  
   - **Key Benefits**: Develops upper body strength (chest, shoulders, triceps) and core stability.
   - **Execution**: Start in a plank position with hands slightly wider than shoulders. Lower your body until your chest approaches the ground, then push back up.
   - **Variations**: Incline, decline, or weighted push-ups.
